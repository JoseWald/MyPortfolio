MySHOP (August 2024)

An inventory management that primarly made by me and Harena Raph to help the supermaket cashier.
This web app will be usefull to them that they can efficiently:
- Watch their daily and weekly revenue
-Make the bill automaticly without calculating anything
-Notice the most and the least selled product
-Notice the product quantity in the stock
-Take a look at the sale history in case of complaint

  • React code: https://github.com/JoseWald/MyShop-VIEW
