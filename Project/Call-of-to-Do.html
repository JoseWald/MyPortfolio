Call of to do

After completing the advanced 8-hour Unity crash course on FreeCodeCamp, I finally created my first 3D First-Person Shooter game. Inspired by Call of Duty: Zombies, the game challenges you to survive against relentless monsters using a Kalashnikov, a revolver, and an axe. The enemies are powered by AI, capable of smelling and tracking your location, making every encounter intense and unpredictable.
